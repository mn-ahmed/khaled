Key Highlights

Action Bar

Manufacturing Orders Management Was Never So Easy!

  • Duration

    • Clicking the Duration icon on the action bar, you can see the scheduled number of days for your different manufacturing orders.

  • Grid

    • Clicking the Grid icon on the action bar, you can get information about the title, duration, start date, and owner of the order. You can also edit or delete any order from the grid. By again clicking on Grid view, it can temporarily hide your order details in case of bulk data and you can have a better view of your orders.

  • Resource Panel

    • Resource Panel feature displays the resources working on an order showing details about their working hours and tasks assigned to them.

  • Zoom To Fit

    • Through Zoom to Fit feature, you can change the scaling of time, which provides you with better visualization and helps in managing your orders in the Manufacturing Gantt Chart. Also, you can view all your orders at once with this feature.

  • Critical Path

    • Critical Path feature highlights all the orders linked with your last order, i.e., it shows the dependency of other tasks on your last task.

  • Days Off

    • Days Off features tells about the availability and unavailability of your resources by highlighting the days or time when a resource is available.

  • Today Button

    • On the right side of the action bar, you’ll see a Today option that shows the orders details of your ongoing day.

  • Time Sorting

    • Time Sorting allows the users to sort the orders in several time patterns such as day, week, month, quarter, etc. There is a Today-marker that highlights the graphical section in the Gantt view and reflects the current day.

  • Sorting

    • There is a Sort icon that is used in sorting the orders/tasks according to name (ascending or descending wise), new to old, and duration (ascending or descending wise).

  • Zoom In

    • At the extreme right of the action bar, there is a full-screen option (represented as a double-pointed arrow), which helps you to view your tasks in full-screen mode making the Manufacturing Gantt Chart full size on the screen.

Gantt View

Modify The View As Per Your Wish!

  • Create

    • The Create button helps to create a new order as you fill the required fields in the order form.

  • Export

    • The Gantt View Time Off report can be exported in various formats like Excel, PDF, PNG, JSON, MS Project(XML), Primavera P6, and iCal.

  • Order Tooltip

    • The Hover Tooltip feature displays the order details such as Start Date & the End Date of the orders as the mouse is moved over a order-bar in the Gantt View . There is also an edit & delete option to modify or delete the order.

  • Order Color

    • With the State Color option, you can select the color of an order according to its current manufacturing state like draft, confirmed, or in progress. The order will then be visualized in the chart in that particular color.

  • Keyboard Shortcuts

    • There are several Keyboard shortcuts that can be used to perform the tasks that one has to perform manually using the functionalities of Gantt View.

Gantt Settings

Customize The Gantt View

  • Tooltip Settings

    • Using Tooltip Settings, you can customize the information that you want to see about the orders in the chart.

  • Dynamic Text

    • By enabling the Dynamic Text in Gantt Chart Settings, you can smartly adjust any long order title into a small bar on the chart.

  • Quick Info

    • Quick info feature in Gantt Chart Settings displays the name, the start date, and the end date of an order on opening it while giving you edit and delete options with it. You can disable this option as well.

Q.1. What is Gantt View Manufacturing?

Gantt View Manufacturing is a module that acts as a centralized platform to manage different manufacturing tasks at once with time. It provides a Gantt View in manufacturing section that makes managing different orders easy within their assigned time period.

Q.2. How to Make a Bar Chart Full Size on the Screen?

In Manufacturing Orders, click at the full-screen option (represented by a double-pointed arrow) at the extreme right corner of the action bar to view your tasks in full-screen mode and make a bar chart full size on the screen.

Q.3. Is This App Compatible with Odoo Enterprise & Can It Be Used As a Manufacturing App For Odoo?

No, it isn’t compatible with Odoo Enterprise at the moment! But, it would soon be compatible with it through future updates and then it can be used as a manufacturing app for Odoo.

Q.4. Do You Provide Any Free Support?

Yes, Ksolves India Limited provides free support for 120 days from the day you purchased the app regarding any hitches, issues, etc.

Q.5. What Is the Difference Between Zoom to Fit and Zoom In?

Through the Zoom to Fit feature, you can change the scaling of time, which provides you with better visualization and helps in managing your orders. Also, you can view all your orders at once with this feature. Zoom-in feature simply helps to magnify the screen of your system, i.e., increase the overall size of your screen.

Q.6. Whom to Contact for Some Customization in the App?

To get customization in your Gantt View Manufacturing module, you can directly contact us as we are the developers of this app. We’ll first check whether your customization request is feasible and then will definitely proceed on it.

Q.7. Is It Possible to Customize a Manufacturing Order?

Yes, it is possible to customize any manufacturing order by setting your preferences in the Settings. You can customize the whole menu by clicking on Gantt Settings in the Configuration on the main menu of the Gantt View Manufacturing module.

Q.8. Can I Create a Dynamic Title for Gantt View Manufacturing?

Yes. By enabling the Dynamic Text in Gantt Chart Settings, you can smartly adjust any long title of the order into a small bar on the chart.

Q.9. How can I Unschedule an Order?

To unschedule an order, first click on it to open it; a tab shows up. In the Components section of this tab, you can unschedule that order.

Q.10. Where Can I Link My Orders with One Another?

In ‘view ks gantt’ at the extreme right of list views in Manufacturing Orders, you can schedule your orders, and increase or decrease their time easily by dragging the dates. The start and end dates of your task will get saved in the database of the module. You can then easily link one order to the other in this view.
